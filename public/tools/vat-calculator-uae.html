La TVA (VAT) aux Émirats Arabes Unis

La VAT (Value Added Tax) a été introduite aux UAE le 1er janvier 2018 au taux standard de 5%, l'un des plus bas au monde. Elle est administrée par la Federal Tax Authority (FTA) selon le Federal Decree-Law No. 8 of 2017.

Les 3 taux possibles

  • 5% standard : biens et services généraux
  • 0% zero-rated : exports hors GCC, transport international, médicaments, premier transfert immobilier résidentiel neuf, éducation et santé sous conditions
  • Exempt : services financiers non rémunérés explicitement, location résidentielle long terme, transport local de passagers

Le Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM)

Le Reverse Charge s'applique notamment aux importations de services depuis l'étranger : le client UAE doit s'auto-liquider la VAT (sortie) et la déduire (entrée) sur la même déclaration. Neutre en trésorerie si pro-rata 100%.

VAT in the United Arab Emirates

VAT was introduced in the UAE on 1 January 2018 at a standard rate of 5%, one of the lowest worldwide. It is administered by the Federal Tax Authority (FTA) under Federal Decree-Law No. 8 of 2017.

The 3 possible rates

  • 5% standard: most goods and services
  • 0% zero-rated: exports outside the GCC, international transport, medicines, first supply of new residential real estate, education and healthcare under conditions
  • Exempt: unremunerated financial services, long-term residential rentals, local passenger transport

Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM)

Reverse Charge notably applies to services imported from abroad: the UAE client self-assesses output VAT and reclaims input VAT on the same return. Cash-neutral if pro-rata is 100%.

FAQ

At what turnover must I register?+
Mandatory above AED 375,000 taxable turnover over 12 months. Voluntary registration from AED 187,500.
How often must I file?+
Quarterly by default (Form 201), monthly above AED 150M turnover. Deadline: 28 days after period end.
